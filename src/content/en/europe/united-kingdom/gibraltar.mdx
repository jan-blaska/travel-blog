  <MainHeader>The Rock at the End of Europe</MainHeader>
  <ImageNextToText imageSrc="/adventures/europe/united-kingdom/gibraltar/WP_20180708_14_07_09_Pro.jpg" imageAlt="Gibraltar macaques" reverse={true} imageWidth="narrow" maxHeight="450px">
    <span className="text-lg md:text-xl text-(--green) font-bold">Airport Runway, Rock and Macaques</span>
    <br />
    I visited Gibraltar by car. To even get into the territory I first had to drive across the airport runway — which was a very unusual experience for me.
    <br />
    There are two ways to visit the famous Rock: either by cable car or on foot to the summit. I chose to walk up. The path is not particularly difficult and the view from the top is truly breathtaking — you can see both the Mediterranean and the Atlantic, and in good weather even the coast of Africa.
    <br />
    What I particularly enjoyed there were the monkeys — the so-called Barbary macaques. Although they look cute and friendly, they can also be dangerous, especially if you have food or a water bottle with you. So you need to be very careful, otherwise you will quickly lose your snack. Overall I would really recommend a visit to Gibraltar to everyone.
