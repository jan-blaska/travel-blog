    <span className="text-xl md:text-2xl">
      When I was doing my Erasmus in Finland, I decided to go on a big adventure. I was particularly interested in southern Finland, especially the cities of Tampere, Turku, and the nearby islands.
    </span>
    <br /><br />
    <span className="text-xl md:text-2xl">
      After a little research I decided on an adventure with my beloved bicycle. What I especially loved in Finland were the free ferries that connect the various islands. During this trip too I made great use of them.
    </span>
  </ImageNextToText>
</Section>
