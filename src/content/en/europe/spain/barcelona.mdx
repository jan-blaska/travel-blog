<Section>
  <MainHeader>La Sagrada Família and the City</MainHeader>
  <ImageNextToText imageSrc="/adventures/europe/spain/barcelona/20220131_120100.jpg" imageAlt="Sagrada Família">
    <span className="text-lg md:text-xl text-(--green) font-bold">Gaudí, Museums and Seven Unforgettable Days</span>
    <br />
    I was particularly impressed by Antoni Gaudí's cathedral — La Sagrada Família. This building is truly unique and fascinated me greatly. The architecture is like nothing else you will see in Europe. Besides the Sagrada Família there are also many interesting museums that are well worth visiting. The food, the culture, and the street life impressed me greatly. In total I spent seven days in Barcelona — and enjoyed every single one of them.
