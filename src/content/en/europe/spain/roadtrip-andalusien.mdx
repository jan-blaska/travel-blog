  <MainHeader>Ronda and the Caminito del Rey</MainHeader>
  <ImageNextToText imageSrc="/adventures/europe/spain/roadtrip-andalusia/WP_20180707_13_35_50_Pro.jpg" imageAlt="Ronda, Andalusia">
    <span className="text-lg md:text-xl text-(--green) font-bold">The Village Above the Gorge</span>
    <br />
    Besides the large cities, Andalusia also has many small villages worth visiting. One of these was Ronda, known for its impressive bridge over the gorge. The view from there is spectacular and the place has its own unique charm.
    <br />
    <span className="text-lg md:text-xl text-(--green) font-bold">Caminito del Rey</span>
    What you absolutely must visit if you love adventure is the Caminito del Rey. This path was once considered very dangerous, but after renovation it is now safe — and the view along the route is simply breathtaking**.**
