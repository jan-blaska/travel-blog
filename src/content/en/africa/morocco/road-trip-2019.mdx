<Section>

    <span className="text-lg md:text-xl text-(--green) font-bold">The Film City and the Skoura Oasis</span>
    <br />
    Our first stop was the city of Ouarzazate, where many Hollywood films have been shot. Not far from there is an oasis called the Skoura Oasis. Then we continued on to the Merzouga desert. I felt like I was in a dream — this exact place had been on my wish list for a long time. A real desert with high dunes and camels.

<Section>
  <MainHeader>The Merzouga Desert</MainHeader>
  <ImageNextToText imageSrc="/adventures/africa/morocco/road-trip-2019/20190212_163244.jpg" imageAlt="Merzouga desert, Morocco" maxHeight="650px">
    <span className="text-lg md:text-xl text-(--green) font-bold">Camel Ride, Stars and Moroccan Music</span>
    <br />
    Our plan was to spend one night in a desert camp at Merzouga. First we went on a camel ride that lasted about two hours. After that we could enjoy the evening. The stars in the desert were incredible. The food prepared for us was delicious, and at the end we danced to Moroccan music.
    <br />
    The next morning we had to return to our car and slowly made our way back towards Marrakech. We truly enjoyed our time in the Merzouga desert.
