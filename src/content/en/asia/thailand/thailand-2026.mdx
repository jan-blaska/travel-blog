      I had been wanting to visit famous Thailand for a long time, and I finally had the opportunity because I found a very good flight ticket. It was truly incredible: a direct flight from Prague to Krabi. I never expected that. The flight took only about ten to twelve hours, which was really pleasant, and the price was surprisingly affordable. I was very lucky to find this last-minute ticket. I was well prepared and already had a rough plan at home of which places I wanted to visit.

  <MainHeader>Krabi & the Koh Hong Islands</MainHeader>
  <ImageNextToText imageSrc="/adventures/asia/thailand/20260308_175303.jpg" imageAlt="Krabi, Thailand">
    <span className="text-lg md:text-xl text-(--green) font-bold">Tiger Cave Temple & Ao Nang</span>
    <br />
    Of course I first had to adjust to the new time zone. Honestly, the first two to three days were quite exhausting until I fully adapted. During that time I explored Krabi and also some interesting places in the area, for example the Tiger Cave Temple. I had to climb many hours on foot to get there, but in the end I was rewarded with a breathtaking view.
    <br />
    During the climb, a group of monkeys accompanied me and other tourists. They looked cute but could also be dangerous. I had no problems with them myself, but it often happens that they steal things from people, such as water bottles. So you need to be careful.

<Section>
  <MainHeader>Phi Phi Islands & Maya Bay</MainHeader>
  <ImageNextToText imageSrc="/adventures/asia/thailand/20260311_181017.jpg" imageAlt="Phi Phi Islands, Thailand" reverse={true} imageWidth="wide">
    <span className="text-lg md:text-xl text-(--green) font-bold">The Famous Beach</span>
    <br />
    From Ao Nang I continued to the Phi Phi Islands, where I spent two nights. The Phi Phi Islands are perfect if you want to visit the famous Maya Bay beach. I spent about two hours there in total, and at one moment the beach was not even overcrowded.
    <br />
    Of course we also enjoyed snorkeling and the fascinating underwater world. The conditions for that were truly excellent. We also experienced beautiful sunsets.

<Section>
  <MainHeader>Koh Lanta</MainHeader>
  <ImageNextToText imageSrc="/adventures/asia/thailand/20260316_164546.jpg" imageAlt="Koh Lanta, Thailand">
    <span className="text-lg md:text-xl text-(--green) font-bold">Scooter, Elephants and Snorkeling</span>
    <br />
    After the Phi Phi Islands I took the ferry on to Koh Lanta. There I rented a scooter to explore the island better. I visited almost all the interesting places on the island and enjoyed the relaxed atmosphere.
    <br />
    The highlight of my time on Koh Lanta was visiting the Following Giants Koh Lanta Ethical Elephant Sanctuary, where I could see many elephants up close. This experience was something truly special for me.
    <br />
    Overall I felt very comfortable on Koh Lanta. The nature was breathtaking, I took several boat trips, enjoyed life beneath the surface while snorkeling, and covered many kilometers by scooter. Unfortunately time passed much too quickly and I had to return to Krabi to then fly back to Central Europe.
